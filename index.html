Becoming a Lawyer

...while finding myself







Law can be a very rewarding field.

A subtitle

I love the idea of law, the challenge it brings, and the problem-solving methods it requires.  It can be frustrating when there is no "right" answer, but comforting to know that that arbitrariness keeps our society from heading too far in an one extreme direction.  My most notable achievements have included a high personal ranking at the National Health Law Moot Court Competition, membership on the Journal of High Technology, and participation in the Clinical Programs. Find out more about my legal life here.

Even still, law school is no picnic.

A subtitle

It takes so much effort, time, expense, and hard work to make it worth  it.  While I have loved the challenge that law school brings, I realize everyday how much it requires. This is why, I make sure to take time for myself and indulge my interests every now and then. Gotta stay human.

In times of stress, it never hurts to have hobbies or passions outside the field. I have learned that balance and stress relief are very important to surviving the law school life style.
